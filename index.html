Frequently Asked Questions

How does my Personal Shopper know what to send me? +
Your Personal Shopper curates items based on your unique preferences, which you share through a style quiz or profile when you sign up. This includes details like your size, favorite, style inspirations, budget, and specific clothing needs or occasions. The more information you provide, the better they can understand your taste. Some services also offer direct communication, so you can give additional feedback or updates as your style evolves. They may also analyze recent fashion trends and your past feedback on items to refine and improve your selections with each shipment.
How much does each box cost? +
The cost of each personal shopper box varies depending on the service and the items you choose to keep. Typically, you'll pay a styling fee ranging from 105 till 280 euros.
What sizes are available? +
Personal shopper services cater to a wide range of sizes to accommodate diverse body types. For instance, Stitch Fix offers women's sizes from 0 to 24 and men's sizes from XS to 3XL. Additionally, services like Dia & Co specialize in plus-size fashion, offering sizes from 14 to 32. This inclusivity ensures that clients receive personalized selections that fit their unique measurements and style preferences.
Can I see the parts before receiving them? +
This answer is simple: No. It’s meant to be a surprise!
How long do I have to try on the pieces? +
Personal shopper services provide a try-on period of approximately seven days.
Are shipping free? What about returns? +
Typically, you'll pay a shipping fee ranging from 4.99 to 40 euros per shipment, which is often credited toward any purchases you make. Same for returns.
